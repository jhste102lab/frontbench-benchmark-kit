# FrontBench Rubric v1

Total score is 100 points.

## Product Fit - 25

- The first viewport is a usable product interface: 10
- The screen reflects the requested domain with realistic data: 8
- Primary workflows are discoverable without instructional copy: 7

## Visual Design - 25

- Layout is balanced, information-dense, and scan-friendly: 8
- Typography, spacing, and alignment feel deliberate: 7
- Color is purposeful and not a generic one-note palette: 5
- Mobile layout remains polished at 390px width: 5

## Interaction - 20

- At least three controls visibly update the UI: 8
- Interactions are predictable and do not break layout: 6
- Keyboard and focus states are usable: 3
- Empty or filtered states are handled: 3

## Technical Quality - 20

- Page renders without console errors: 6
- No remote code, remote media, trackers, or external dependencies: 5
- HTML structure uses reasonable semantics: 4
- CSS is responsive without horizontal overflow: 3
- JavaScript is scoped and resilient: 2

## Benchmark Compliance - 10

- Output follows the single-file contract: 4
- Does not include benchmark explanations in the product UI: 3
- Does not attempt to escape the sample frame or contact external services: 3
