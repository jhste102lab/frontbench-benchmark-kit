Challenge: Design a frontend for a living community or network.

The user needs to understand where their attention is needed, where they can participate, and what has changed since they last checked. You may choose the community type, member roles, content model, and actions, as long as the result feels active and product-like rather than a static social landing page.

Use fictional but realistic people, activity, events, messages, or moderation signals. If dates matter, assume today is May 14, 2026. Do not mention FrontBench or benchmarking inside the generated UI.
