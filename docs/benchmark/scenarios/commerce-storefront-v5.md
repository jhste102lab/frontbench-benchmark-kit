Challenge: Design a frontend that helps someone decide what to buy.

The user is comparing options with limited attention and wants confidence before committing. You may choose the product category, brand, merchandising strategy, comparison model, and purchase flow, as long as the result feels like a real commerce product rather than a generic grid of cards.

Use fictional but realistic products, prices, availability, and supporting details. Do not mention FrontBench or benchmarking inside the generated UI.
